Pancakes

Description

Traditional Swedish pancakes!

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs
  • 2 cups milk
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon vegetable oil, or as needed

Steps

  1. Beat eggs in a large bowl; add milk, flour, sugar, 1 tablespoon vegetable oil, and vanilla extract and mix until batter is very thin.

  2. a griddle or a Swedish plett pan over medium heat; add about 1 teaspoon oil. Pour a small amount of batter onto the griddle and griddle to spread the batter into a thin layer; cook until lightly browned, 2 to 3 minutes. Flip pancake and cook until browned on other side, 2 to 3 minutes more. Roll pancake and place on a plate. Repeat with remaining batter.
